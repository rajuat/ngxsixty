X60 is a lifestyle.

A healthy lifestyle that involves eating right, exercising regularly & sleeping well so you become fitter, healthier & Stronger ...... Everyday.

Eat Right

i.e. meat/ vegetables, nuts & seeds, some fruits, little starch and no sugar. Outsource the headache of measuring, preparing and monitoring your intake to levels that will support exercise but not body fat.

Exercise Regularly

Make inactivity your enemy. Keep workouts short and intense for dramatic gains in fitness. Spend 60 mins a day at our certified CrossFit centre for a high intensity, constantly varied functional workout. Certified trainers & peers will guarantee its competitive & fun

Sleep Well

One less hour of sleep does not equal an extra hour of achievement or enjoyment. Rest longer today to get more done tomorrow. Allow us to monitor your sleep patterns and share simple tips that will greatly enhance your quality of sleep.
